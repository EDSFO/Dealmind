# Diio - Reference Product

**URL:** https://diio.com/br
**Date:** 2025-12-30

## Product Overview

**Diio** is an AI assistant for sales that analyzes conversations and helps you prioritize, act quickly, and make better decisions.

## Core Features

### 1. Conversation Analysis
- Analyzes video calls, phone calls, WhatsApp, and LinkedIn messages
- Detects: interests, objections, progress/risk signals, commitments
- Identifies patterns and phrases that work
- Highlights blind spots

### 2. CRM Auto-Update
- Captures next steps, pain points, and commitments automatically
- Completes CRM entries while team focuses on conversations
- Saves time on manual data entry

### 3. Actionable Insights
- Proposes clear next actions
- Helps prioritize deals based on conversation signals
- Predicts conversation success likelihood

### 4. Team Collaboration
- Review, comment, and share conversations
- Align team on customer feedback
- Standardize sales discourse
- Avoid repeating efforts

### 5. Conversation Intelligence
- Search and ask questions about any conversation (like a crystal ball)
- Understand customer objections and friction points
- Improve sales processes

## Use Cases

- **Prospecting (SDR):** Identify interests and objections from first contact
- **Sales:** Free up operational time for closing deals
- **Customer Success:** Summarize important points and alert about risks
- **Recruiting:** Clearer, comparable interviews with less bias
- **Product:** Collect direct customer feedback without attending every meeting
